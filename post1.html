Shanghai - A location that everyone should visit once in their life time

Published October 14, 2025

Shanghai, a bustling metropolis in China, is a city that seamlessly blends the old with the new. From its historic Bund waterfront to the futuristic skyline of Pudong, Shanghai offers a unique experience for every traveler.

Another must-visit place in Shanghai is the Yuyuan Garden, where you can take a stroll in a stunning Ming dynasty garden (built in 1559). The garden actually fills almost half of a large block, straight in the center of Shanghai.

Lastly, you simply cannot talk about China or Shanghai without mentioning the food. From street food stalls to high-end restaurants, Shanghai offers a culinary adventure that is sure to delight your taste buds. Be sure to try local specialties like Xiaolongbao (soup dumplings) and Shengjianbao (pan-fried buns).

Shanghai really is a city that has something for everyone.
